Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a quote?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form above, calling us directly, or visiting our showroom in Indiranagar. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours with a detailed quote.

What areas do you service?

We primarily service Bengaluru, Chennai, and Coimbatore, but we can take on projects throughout India. For locations outside our primary service areas, additional charges may apply.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard home interior project typically takes 45-60 days from design approval to completion. Commercial projects may take longer depending on requirements.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, all our products come with a standard warranty period ranging from 1-5 years depending on the product category. We also offer extended warranty options for additional peace of mind.
